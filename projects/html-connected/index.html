Services

Whatever your needs, whether you are a first-time homebuyer or a long-time homeowner, we've got you covered.

Purchasing a home is a significant step in everyone's life and can be an extensive process. We are here to walk you through each step, offering home loan expertise to empower you from the minute we first speak to when you receive your keys.

Is it time to re-examine your homeownership and financial goals? Whether you are a new homeowner or have been paying on your mortgage for a longer period, it may be time to ensure that your mortgage still fits your needs. Take the first step and connect with us for a free consultation. During our meeting, we will walk you through the refinancing process and show you how to maximize the benefits of refinancing your home loan.

Common Reasons to Refinance Your Current Mortgage:

  • Take advantage of current market rates
  • Lower your monthly payment
  • Change from an adjustable-rate loan to a fixed-rate loan
  • Adjust the term of the loan for your benefit
  • Consolidate high-interest debt with a cash-out refinance

If you are looking for a loan with major purchasing power, then consider a Conventional loan. This versatile loan varies on the required down payment but allows for the purchase of primary homes, vacation homes, and investment properties. Contact us to find out more about the many options open to you with Conventional loans. 

We want to help you achieve your homeownership goals, no matter your financial situation. FHA (Federal Housing Administration) loans provide the opportunity to enter the real estate market if you have limited funds for a down payment or need flexible options to qualify for a home, such as a lower credit score.

Connected Home Lending Partners would like to thank you for your service and honor you by providing VA (Veteran's Affairs) loans. If you are a veteran, active-duty personnel, or surviving spouse of a veteran, consider using your VA benefit to finance your home purchase. This loan requires no down payment and no mortgage insurance which allows for lower monthly payments

If your homeownership dream is set in the idyllic countryside, then you may be a perfect fit for a USDA loan. With this loan backed by the United States Department of Agriculture, you can purchase a home in qualifying rural areas with zero down payment and the added possibility of financing your closing costs.

If you're in the market to upgrade your current home or purchase an investment or second home, then consider the benefits of jumbo loan financing. These loans provide borrowers, including first-time homeowners, with options to finance $550,000 or more. Connected Home Lending Partners offers our clients a wider range of options with larger loan amounts. We're here to help you move into your dream home, no matter the size.

If you are age 62 or over, it may be time to consider a reverse mortgage. Through a reverse mortgage, you can convert a portion of your home's equity into tax-free money. You can use this money for debt consolidation, home improvements, wealth management, or even a vacation. You've earned it.

A bridge loan is a short-term loan used until a person or company secures permanent financing or removes an existing obligation. It allows the user to meet current obligations by providing immediate cash flow. Bridge loans are short term, and are usually backed by some form of collateral, such as property.

Are you considering a move when you retire, either to find a home that better meets your retirement needs or improve your location? With a Reverse for Purchase loan, you can:
  • Free up your cash flow - no payments are required on a reverse mortgage. You only have to pay your taxes, homeowners insurance, and other required home expenses.
  • Extend the of life of your IRAs and other investment assets
  • Qualify for a home using the more unique and minimal income and credit requirements
  • Increase your purchasing power to buy the home you really want

Whether upsizing, downsizing, or right-sizing, the choice is yours and the possibilities are endless.
