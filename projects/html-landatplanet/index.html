PLANET WITH A PURPOSE

Planet with a Purpose is Planet Financial Group’s environmental and social responsibility platform. Planet With a Purpose supports our planet, people, and their prosperity – every single day. Planet with a Purpose reflects Planet’s commitment to fostering growth in the communities we serve.

Over the past 4 years, Planet has contributed the following locally and nationally:

  • Funded the planting of more than a quarter-million trees in our National Forests by the National Forest Foundation.
  • Fed hungry families, reduced food waste and offset carbon emissions via The Farmlink Project.
  • Supported our nation’s veterans and those who currently serve by endowing a scholarship administered by the Army Scholarship Foundation.
