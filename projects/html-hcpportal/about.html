About Us

A Platform to Help HCPs Manage Medical Content and Medical Education

The Life Sciences Ecosystem generates more new knowledge on a daily basis than any other industry and Healthcare Providers (HCPs) are tasked with engaging this constant stream of information. HCPportals.com was created as a type of personal knowledge assistant for HCPs, providing a growing suite of tools to reduce the time they spend finding relevant information, engaging it and sharing it amongst their peers.

A Platform for the Life Science Organizations Providing Valued Treatments

Pharmaceuticals, Biotechs and Medical Device companies are at the forefront of developing new treatments with Medical Affairs departments responsible for bringing new knowledge to HCPs. HCPportals.com was designed as a fully integrated portal network to support MSLs and Clinical Education professionals meet a wide variety of needs across their organization.

A Platform for Patient Support Material Distribution, Patient Education and Educational Intervention Delivery

With HCPportals.com, centralized organizations can build patient educational interventions which can then be shared out to health system portals and practice portals for patient assignment. This design gives larger organizations with the resources to design and support the development of patient educational tools the ability to craft these interventions, while maintaining patient privacy. Engagement data is anonymized and shared back to the sponsor organizations that designed the intervention for use in Health Economics and Outcomes Research (HEOR).

A Platform for Health Economics & Outcomes Research (HEOR)
