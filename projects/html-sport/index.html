Welcome to Ludusio!

The best option for athletes looking to work with the best to become the best!

Learn more..

Who we are!

The path to greatness begins with one step! Whether you have taken that step or not, Ludusio connects you with various trainers within your area to assist you in refining your sports skills! Whether you are doing this to become the best, or simply for fun and exercise, Ludusio is a community based app that provides an environment and the opportunity to never stop evolving. We challenge YOU to exceed your goals and dominate your sport of choice.

What we Provide

From Basketball, to Lacrosse, or even kickboxing, the sports demonstrated in the SERVICES tab will continue to grow with time. Each coach or trainer will have their specialty sport or sports that they work in.

Our Coaches

Ludusio offers many coaches with experience in multiple sports that are tailored to you based on your location and level of training requested. These coaches are here for you, and want to help you become the best version of yourself!

Book Appointments

Booking an appointment with coaches is easy! We build around your schedule so that your training experience is fully personalized with what makes the most sense for your sport and training level.

Communicate With Your Trainer

Have an easy back-and-forth form of communication with your trainer or coach through our app’s messaging feature, making the process personable. Just chat them up!

Manage Your Bookings

Have a busy schedule? We work around your schedule, so it is easy for you to time your practices or training. You do not have to conform to any requirements, and our coaches are flexible enough to help manage any changes that may occur.

Testimonials

Hear from our satisfied customers and trainers who have worked with us. We hope to see your name in our valued customers list!
