About Broken Hands

Broken Hands was founded in 2017 with the sole aim of taking server-side Swift mainstream.

Founder - Tim Condon

Tim is the founder of Broken Hands. He's been involved with Server-Side Swift and Vapor since the very early days. These days he is part of the Vapor Core Team and sits on the Swift Server Workgroup and Swift Website Workgroup. Tim speaks at conferences around the world and delivered training courses on Vapor and server-side Swift in four continents.

Broken Hands now consists of a dedicated team of developers working on numerous client projects. We look after clients around the world, both big and small providing consultancy services, contract work and advisory services. We use both AWS and Google Cloud for deploying projects and maintain several client projects and offer a variety of SLAs to suit different needs.
