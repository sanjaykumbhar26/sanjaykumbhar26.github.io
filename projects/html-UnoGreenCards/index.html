What is V-Card?

It’s an NFC-enabled card with sharing information with just a tap. A digital business/personal card. A samll initiative by us to make the earth greener by not using the traditional paper business cards and switching to our Digital card wgich is not only eco friendly but saves tons of rupees that we spent on printing paper cards.

A online digital card is a virtual representation of your profile which includes your Personal, Professional, Business, Social information and many more.

Digital Cards products are the unique product in Digital Marketing Industries

Online Digital Card allows the world to get in touch with you in a more simpler and convenient way, enabling everyone to share their information under one link.

How it works?

For NFC integrated Phones

  • Android or Any Other:

    Go to Settings Search for “NFC” Enable NFC

  • Iphone or IOS

    No need to do anything

For NFC integrated Phones

  • Android or Any Other:

    Open Camera and scan QR Code OR Scan QR code from Google lens

  • Android or Any Other:

    No need to do anything
